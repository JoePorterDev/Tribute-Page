Joshua Michael Homme

Musician. Songwriter. Badass.
Joshua Homme (pictured above) performing with rock band Queens of the Stone Age, look at that smile!

    A timeline of his life and career:

  • 1973 - Carlo Von Sexron is born in Joshua Tree, Califonia.
  • 1982 - He buys his first guitar and start taking polka guitar lessons... yeah.
  • 1987 - 1995 - He starts his first (cult) successful band, Kyuss. They make 4 highly praised, awful selling albums then break up.
  • 1997 - Queens of the Stone Age is born!
  • 1998 - 2008 - QOTSA create 5 albums, all more successful than the other, and do lots (and I mean LOTS) of touring.
  • 2009 - Josh starts supergroup Them Crooked Vultures with Dave Grohl and John Paul Jones (what a lineup!), make an album, and tour it.
  • 2013 - Present - QOTSA make 2 more albums, both more successful than the rest (show off), and continue touring and playing to this day.

"If you expect anything from music, you expect too much."

-- Joshua Homme with advice for other musicians
